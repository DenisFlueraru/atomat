ATOMAT LAB — Tools that listen
Founded in 2021, ATOMAT LAB explores the subtle textures of sound, vibration, and electromagnetism. Every tool is handcrafted with functional simplicity and timeless aesthetics.

TRT — Texture Recording Tool: Developed from experimental research, it captures the physical textures of surfaces and transforms them into sound.

It’s the foundation for our handmade audio tools used by sound designers and experimental artists worldwide.

FIELD5 — Phantom-powered contact microphone: Modular and versatile, designed for immersive field recordings and experimental setups.

We also run workshops to build microphones, explore field textures, and create collaborative audio pieces. Learn more at denisflueraru.com.
